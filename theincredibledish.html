The Incredible Dish

Description

This dish is best described as a combination of incredibleness, and awesomeness! Many talented chefs around the world have tried to recreate it, but only one has the talent to - Edna from the Incredibles!!!

Ingredients

  • Basil
  • Thyme
  • Chicken tenders
  • Lemons
  • Oil (crude and cooking oil)
  • Comprehensive life insurance
  • Vanilla extract
  • Cinnamon
  • Garlic
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Onion

Steps

  1. Squeeze the lemon until 100mL of lemon juice has been extracted
  2. Gently pour all herbs, garnishes, and seasonings into the lemon extract
  3. Add mixture to a large pot and heat to a boil
  4. Add vanilla extract and wait 3 minutes
  5. Add crude oil to mixture and wait until combustion begins
  6. When pot has combusted, add all remaining ingredients
  7. Wait until all ingredients have been burnt
  8. Enjoy the crispy Incredible Dish ash!
